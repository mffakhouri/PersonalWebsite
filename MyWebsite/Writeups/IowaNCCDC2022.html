Iowa State NCCDC 2022

Iowa State University invited surrounding Universities to a national Cyber Defense Competition that they host every year. The purpose of this competition is to provide students with a simulation of real-life experiences in Information Assurance for the purpose of education. Students play the role of the Blue Team (or, the Information Assurance community), under the influence of an active Red Team (users simulating threat actors). Teams are scored on a variety of factors, including: documentation, network usability/integrity, and service uptime.
